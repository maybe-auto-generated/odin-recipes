Pesto Pasta

Description

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Start with your favorite pasta shape.
  • Onion and oil: Cook the onion in olive oil until itâ€™s translucent.
  • Pesto: Use store-bought or homemade pesto sauce.
  • Seasonings: This pesto pasta is simply seasoned with salt and pepper.
  • Cheese: Grate your own Parmesan cheese instead of using the pre-shredded stuff.

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil the pasta in salted water until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes, and drain.
  2. Make the pesto mixture: Cook the onion in oil until softened, then stir in the pesto and seasonings utnil warmed through.
  3. Assemble the pasta: Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
